ABOUT HCL

INTRODUCTION

 HCL was launched in 1976 with an Rs. 187,000 investment, With the launching of Far East Computers in Singapore in 1980, HCL penetrated the international market to supply IT gear. In its first year, the organization made a profit of Rs 1 million. The first three subsidiaries of HCL Organisation were: HCL Technologies was established in 1991 as a branch of HCL's R&d department. HCL Infosystems is a company that offers information technology services. HCL Healthcare is a healthcare company located in the United. Headed by Shiv Nadar, a group of former Delhi Cloth & General Mills employees set up a computer manufacturing firm in 1976. In their first product, Nadar and his team, which comprised Arjun Malhotra, Ajai Chowdhry, D.S. Puri, Yogesh Vaidya, and Subhash Arora began selling teledigital calculators to raise money for their main product. the Company was renamed Hindustan Computers Limited (HCL) on August 11, 1976.

FOUNDATION

As a technology development business, HCL Overseas Limited was founded on November 12th, 1991. On February 10, 1992, the company acquired its certificate of incorporation and commenced business. the company was renamed HCL Technologies Limited in October 1999, two years just after the company's name was changed to HCL Consulting Limited in July 1994. HCL Technologies is one of HCL Corporation's four businesses, with HCL Infosystems being the other. HCL Healthcare was launched in February 2014. A new initiative of HCL Corporation, HCL TalentCare is the company's fourth venture. Since its inception, HCL Technologies has been operating under the name HCL Overseas Ltd. At the beginning of July 1994, the company's name was changed to "HCL Consulting Limited". The firm was renamed HCL Technologies Limited on October 6, 1999, to "better represent its activities". During the period from 1991 to 1999, the firm developed its software development capabilities into the US, European, and Asia / Pacific markets.

Social responsibility

CORPRATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBLITY

HCL Technologies Limited is committed to strengthening the planet's environmental and socio-economic progress by setting "Corporate Social Responsibility" at the core of its operation. It stands firmly against the exploitation of all living creatures and resources and works relentlessly for the preservation of our environment and respect for all lives, aligned with Global Sustainable Development Goals.

More than 1.5 million individuals have benefited from the company's main CSR programmes, HCL Samuday, HCL Grant, and HCL Uday, in the Financial Year 2019-20. By focusing on Early Childhood Care and Development (ECCD), Education, Health, Livelihoods, Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH), and Disaster Risk Reduction and Response (DRR) in rural and urban areas, HCL Foundation has created opportunities to break the cycle of poverty.

HCL Samuday has been able to create the following improvements on the ground through its initiatives across six verticals for sustainable rural development - agriculture, education, health, infrastructure, livelihood, and WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene).

The HCL Grant is one of India's largest and most sought formalized CSR grants, with the goal of recognising the emergence of Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) and supporting their  projects that reach out to the country's rural communities..

HCL UDAY : HCL Foundation works to break the cycle of urban poverty through its comprehensive urban development initiative, HCL Uday, with a goal of clean, green, healthy, and empowered communities. The program's strategy is founded on the fundamental idea that the urban poor aspire to a life of dignity and respect for themselves. HCL Foundation provides opportunities through this programme by carefully designing tactics that speak to both their surroundings and the community's goals.
